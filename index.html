Why Cars Inc?

Cars Inc. is a certified installer of the Paint Protection Film (PPF or Clear Bra). Serving dealerships of the luxury, ultra luxury and performance segment of the automotive industry with such brands as Porsche, Bentley, Lamborghini, Rolls Royce, Ferrari and many more.

We provide the highest quality of PPF installation while utilizing specially designed tools and advanced placement techniques to provide exceptional service to our customers. We don't leave open corners or edges while install PPF on vehicles. We take our time and disassemble small parts from the car which may stay on the way of seamless look.
